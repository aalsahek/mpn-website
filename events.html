OurEvents

A look back at gatherings, workshops, and community moments organized by MPN Finland.

All Events

Career Networking Night

Professionals across tech, healthcare, and design shared insights on career growth, job hunting, and authentic leadership. Guided conversations and light mentoring helped attendees expand networks and identify next steps with confidence.

Leadership Workshop

An interactive deep dive into values-based leadership. Participants practiced decision-making, reflection, and practical frameworks for leading teams while maintaining integrity and balance.

Tech Mentoring Circle

Senior engineers and product leaders offered guidance on portfolios, interviews, and navigating career transitions. Small-group sessions created space for specific feedback and meaningful connections.

Healthcare Careers Panel

Doctors, researchers, and public health professionals discussed pathways, specialization, and well-being. Attendees explored diverse roles and practical steps to advance with purpose.

Entrepreneurship Meetup

Founders shared stories on validating ideas, acquiring customers, and building resilient teams. The session closed with peer feedback and accountability circles.

Design Roundtable

Designers and product thinkers explored accessibility, inclusive content, and portfolio storytelling. Hands-on prompts sparked practical improvements attendees could ship immediately.

Community Iftar

An evening of reflection and connection during Ramadan with short talks, shared meals, and open conversation. New friendships and collaborations formed across disciplines.

Graduate Mixer

Recent graduates connected with mentors across fields, shared goals, and mapped practical first steps into the workplace with community support.
