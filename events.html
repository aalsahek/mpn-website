Islamic Psychology

This event is designed for professionals and community members seeking a deeper understanding of the self, identity, and resilience through a faith-based lens.

Dr. Rothman is a globally recognized expert, Head of Islamic Psychology at Cambridge Muslim College, and co-founder of the International Association of Islamic Psychology. His work bridges clinical psychology with Islamic tradition to address the unique mental and emotional needs of Muslims today.

Startups & Founders: The Journey

A day dedicated to real stories, bold ventures, and honest conversations. Hosted at Aalto Design Factory, it brought together Muslim professionals and entrepreneurial minds for an inspiring program of keynotes from Fadumo Ali, Sami A., Moyeen Ahmad, and Guleed Ahmed, alongside a meaningful panel discussion and rich conversations.

More than just an event, it offered real insights from founders who have built and scaled businesses, a powerful network of like-minded professionals, a strong program exploring innovation and values-driven leadership, and an uplifting atmosphere with time to recharge and engage. It provided the perfect opportunity to invest in meaningful conversations, build relationships, and strengthen the entrepreneurial spirit within our community.

Your Mind Matters

Your Mind Matters created a safe, open, and stigma-free space to talk honestly about mental health, identity, stress, and burnout. Led by Dr. Hibo and Dr. Ibrahim, both deeply committed to mental well-being and equity in healthcare, the event invited meaningful reflection, vulnerability, and real conversation about how to care for our minds in today’s world.

Filled with warmth, empathy, and connection, it brought together hearts and voices in a powerful space of growth and understanding. A sincere thank you to our amazing speakers whose honesty and compassion made this gathering truly impactful.

Deconstructing AI: Practical Workshop

Deconstructing AI: Practical Workshop was a hands-on introduction to Generative AI, designed to turn curiosity into real capability. Participants explored the essentials, saw practical examples, and took part in guided exercises led by our expert speakers Adan Mohamed and Hussein Ismail, whose experience and passion made complex ideas clear, relevant, and inspiring.

The event brought people together to learn, connect, and immediately apply their new skills through a live challenge. It was approachable for beginners yet deeply valuable for those already working with AI a truly engaging experience and a meaningful way to gather as a community right before Ramadan.

Faith Inspired Leadership

Faith-Inspired Leadership brought together powerful voices to explore how faith, service, and responsibility shape meaningful leadership in today’s world. Through insightful keynotes and a dynamic panel discussion, our distinguished speakers, Hunderra Assefa, Dr. Muhammad Zeeshan, Shabana Ahmadzai, and Dr. Ilkhom Khalimzoda. shared real-life experiences, lessons in purpose-driven leadership, and reflections rooted in values, integrity, and community impact.

The event offered a rich blend of inspiration, learning, and connection, creating space for honest dialogue, reflection, and growth. Participants engaged with thought-provoking ideas, enjoyed meaningful networking, and experienced uplifting community spirit, making this one of our most impactful gatherings to date.

Design Roundtable
