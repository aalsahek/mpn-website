Members

Membership connects you to a growing Muslim professional community in Finland through events, learning, and shared support.

What Membership Offers

Muslim Professionals Network ry is a non-profit community for Muslim professionals in Finland.

We create spaces for networking, mentorship, and professional development, while staying rooted in Islamic values. Our work supports career growth, representation in Finnish working life, and honest dialogue between Muslims and broader Finnish society.

By joining MPN as a member you'll get access to the following benefits:

  • Members-only networking events
  • Priority access to limited-seat events
  • Members-only WhatsApp group for ongoing connection
  • MPN newsletter about relevant topics curated for Muslim professionals
  • Exclusive member offers from MPN's growing partner network

Your first event as a member: our Spring General Assembly will be held on Sunday, 26 April - have your voice in shaping MPN's future!
