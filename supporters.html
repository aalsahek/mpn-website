Partners & Sponsors

Kanzi Coaching

A life coaching service focused on helping busy professionals and young adults gain clarity, routine, and purpose. It combines Islamic worldview principles (Barakah-thinking) with practical coaching methods, focusing on identity, habits, and systems.

Rauhapiiri ry

A Helsinki-based registered youth and family association that supports the personal, social, and spiritual development of children and young people. The organization promotes community, learning, and growth based on values of peace, kindness, and respect.

HUB Finland

Offers versatile co-working, meeting, and event spaces in the heart of Tikkurila. Designed for entrepreneurs and teams, they provide a modern, flexible environment to foster growth and collaboration.
