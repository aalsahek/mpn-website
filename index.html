Muslim Professionals Network

Who We Are

Muslim Professionals Network (MPN) is a non-profit, non-political community of Muslim professionals in Finland. We create spaces for networking, mentorship, and professional development, while staying rooted in Islamic values. Our work supports career growth, representation in Finnish working life, and honest dialogue between Muslims and the wider society.

Support Us

OUR VISION

A Finland where Muslim professionals are seen, respected, and included in every level of working life, and where Islamic values and professional excellence are not in conflict but openly coexist.

OUR MISSION

Create spaces, knowledge, and opportunities for Muslim professionals in Finland. We host events, build connections across industries, share practical guidance for navigating working life, and open doors for collaboration between Muslims and the wider society.
