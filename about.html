AboutMPN

Muslim Professionals Network (MPN) brings Muslim professionals in Finland together to learn, mentor, and make a positive impact. We create accessible spaces, showcase role models, and build bridges across industries.

Our Story

MPN began as a simple idea: create welcoming spaces where Muslim professionals could meet, learn, and uplift one another. What started as informal gatherings quickly evolved into a movement grounded in service, mentorship, and faith-informed values. Through effort and listening, we nurtured trust, sparked collaboration, and inspired confidence across communities.

Today, MPN connects industries, celebrates role models, and champions opportunity, belonging, and integrity across Finland. We design accessible events, foster mentorship, and amplify diverse voices to unlock potential. With partners and volunteers, we turn ideas into action, building bridges, advancing careers, and strengthening faith-aligned leadership for the next generation together.

Our Vision

A thriving network where Muslim professionals in Finland connect, elevate one another, and contribute to a just, prosperous society.

Our Mission

Create opportunities for learning, mentorship, and collaboration; celebrate role models; and build bridges across communities and industries.

Our principles

Three pillars guide everything we do.

Learning

Workshops, career stories, and open resources for continuous growth.

Networking

Spaces to meet, share, and build collaborations.

Empowering

We elevate role models and support one another.
