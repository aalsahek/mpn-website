AboutMPN

Our Story

Muslim Professionals Network began with an idea: to create spaces where Muslim professionals in Finland could truly feel seen, valued, and uplifted. What started in August 2023 as a warm gathering in Nuuksio filled with nature, shared faith, and honest conversations quickly became a powerful moment of recognition, as we realized how deeply our journeys, challenges, hopes, and ambitions connected us.

From those beginnings, MPN has grown into a compassionate and purpose-driven community shaped by care, trust, and intent. By listening, learning together, and supporting one another, we have built bonds that go beyond networking and created spaces where talents are seen and experiences are shared.

Today, we bring together Muslims from diverse backgrounds across Finland, nurturing trust, mentorship, belonging, and meaningful collaboration. With partners, sponsors, volunteers, and supportive communities by our side, we design impactful gatherings, empower voices, and turn intention into impact, opening doors, strengthening identity, and inspiring faith-aligned leadership for the future.

Our Vision

A Finland where Muslim professionals are seen, respected, and included in every level of working life, and where Islamic values and professional excellence are not in conflict but openly coexist.

Our Mission

Create spaces, knowledge, and opportunities for Muslim professionals in Finland. We host events, build connections across industries, share practical guidance for navigating working life, and open doors for collaboration between Muslims and the wider society.

Our principles

Three pillars guide everything we do.

Learning

We create meaningful learning experiences that inspire growth, curiosity, and confidence. Through workshops, conversations, and shared knowledge, we equip Muslim professionals with the tools to thrive personally, professionally, and spiritually.

Networking

We build trusted spaces where genuine connections flourish. By bringing people together across industries, backgrounds, and experiences, we foster collaboration, shared opportunities, and a strong sense of belonging.

Empowering

We elevate voices, celebrate talent, and strengthen identity. With partners, sponsors, volunteers, and supportive communities, we turn ambition into action, opening doors, building leadership, and enabling Muslims in Finland to succeed with confidence and integrity.
